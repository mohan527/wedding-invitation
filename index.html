Saptapadi – The Seven Sacred Vows

“Saptapadi is a significant ritual in Hindu weddings symbolizing the sacred seven steps taken together by the bride and groom. Each step represents a promise that strengthens their lifelong bond.”

Nourishment and Prosperity – The couple promises to provide for each other and share their experiences.
Strength and Health – They vow to support each other in facing life’s challenges and remain healthy.
Wealth and Happiness – The couple promises to share their wealth and happiness equally.
Family and Children – They pledge to nurture their family and welcome children with care.
Seasons and Stability – The couple vows to stay steady through life’s changes and seasons.
Loyalty and Friendship – They commit to being best friends and loyal partners.
Mutual Respect – The couple promises to respect each other and uphold mutual dignity.
